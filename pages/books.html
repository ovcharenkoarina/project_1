My Favourite Books

Bunny

Bunny is a novel by Mona Awad. It's a darkly comedic story about the life of a young woman in a prestigious MFA program.

The Secret History

The Secret History is a novel by Donna Tartt. It follows a group of classics students at an elite college and their descent into moral ambiguity and murder.

Thinking, Fast and Slow

Thinking, Fast and Slow is a book by Daniel Kahneman. It explores how people think, including the two systems of thinking and their impact on decision-making.
